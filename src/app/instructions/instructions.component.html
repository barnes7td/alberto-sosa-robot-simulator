This project implements a simulator for a small robot named Sally (triangle). Sally can move forward and turn left and right. Sally's objective is to find the goal (circle) on the map.

Sally can respond to the following commands:

RESTART - ex:restart
Clear the state and shuffle the goal and robot locations
MOVE - ex:move
Move the robot one step forward
LEFT - ex:left
Turn the robot 90 degrees to the left
RIGHT ex:right
Turn the robot 90 degrees to the right
PLACE X,Y,F - ex:place 1,2,west
Where X is number on x axis, Y is a number on Y axis, and F is north, south, east, or west.
'Warp' the robot to a new position and orientation. This is 'cheating', but useful for debugging.
